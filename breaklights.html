Break Light PCB

Project Overview

Details

The Break Light PCB is a custom-designed circuit board that integrates advanced lighting components to enhance vehicle safety. It features optimized power delivery, compact layout design, and robust testing to ensure reliability.

  • Purpose: Control all the lights for the University of Michigan Solar Car
  • Features: Connectors to allow light connections all around the car as well as a solder jumper to switch the board between a front and back light mode allowing the board to be reused for both applications.
  • Applications: Tail lights, brake lights, and other vehicle signaling systems.
